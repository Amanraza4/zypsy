Approach

Zypsy uniquely integrates with seed to growth stage startups in support of their brand and product design needs.

EQUITY ENGAGEMENT

Shared success

For Seed startups—where potential is more abundant than capital—we invest creative expertise in exchange for equity, providing startups with mainstay access to the design support essential to success. Like a valued co-founder with aligned incentives and mutual commitment to your success.

INTEGRATED APPROACH

Team of integrated product experts

It takes a process-oriented team of embedded experts to effectively navigate each stage of product iteration. Zypsy attracts best-in-class design and engineering talent from around the globe to work with the world’s next-best companies.

FOUNDER FOCUSED

Selective Partnership

We inhabit the startup realm and therefore respect the intense struggle of the entrepreneurial process. We form partnerships selectively and make commitments to our founders—to honor their vision, optimize their experience, and deliver world-class products.

GROWTH ORIENTED

Scalable design support

Zypsy delivers agile expertise to help you scale, eliminating resource-intensive hiring, onboarding, and management, as well as the skillset limitations of individual in-house designers. From your first design hire to your fifth, we meet your multidisciplinary design needs at every stage of the startup spectrum.

STORY DRIVEN

Designed with intention

Our formulary processes guide the evolution of startup identity and product design. Zypsy prioritizes strategy, analytics, and integrity to hone the stories we help tell. From this foundation, we weave authentic and compelling brand and product experiences.

LASTING IMPACT

Long-term relationships

Where most agencies offer short-lived, transactional collaboration, the Zypsy team dovetails with your own for a long-term, integrated partnership. We focus on your success, rather than on deliverables, and in doing so add immeasurable integrity and value to your design output.
